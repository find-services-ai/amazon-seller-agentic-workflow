## 5. Negotiation Targets

| Term | Target | Walk Away | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Unit Price** | $2.50-3.50 | >$4.00 | Must leave room for shipping + margin |
| **MOQ** | 50 units | >100 units | $500 budget / ~$5-6 landed |
| **Dropship Fee** | ≤$0.50/order | >$1.00/order | Erodes margin at low volume |
| **Shipping to US** | ≤$2.50/unit | >$3.50/unit | Affects landed cost critically |
| **Lead Time** | ≤10 days | >15 days | Customer experience on Amazon |
| **Payment Terms** | Trade Assurance, 30% deposit | 100% upfront to unknown | Risk mitigation |
| **Sample Cost** | ≤$15 shipped | >$30 | Test before commit |
| **Defect Policy** | Replace/refund >2% defect | No policy | Quality assurance |

### Counter-Offer Scripts (Ready to Deploy)

**If price quoted >$4.00:**
> "Based on competitive landed costs and my margin requirements, I need to hit $3.50 per unit or lower to make this viable. Can you meet this price at 50 units? I'll scale to 300+ monthly once I validate demand."

**If MOQ quoted >100:**
> "I understand your standard MOQ. However, I operate a test-first model—50 units to validate quality and fit, then scale fast. Can you accommodate 50 units at a slightly higher per-unit price for the first order only?"

**If no dropship offered:**
> "Dropship is essential for my model. Even if you don't advertise it, can you ship individual orders (1-3 units) to US addresses with tracking? I'll pay a reasonable per-order fulfillment fee."
